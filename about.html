About Us

Building the Future of Student Productivity

ScholarSync is a comprehensive academic platform designed to help students organize, learn, and excel in their studies—all in one beautiful interface.

Our Mission

We believe that every student deserves access to powerful, intuitive tools that make learning easier and more effective. ScholarSync combines note-taking, task management, grade tracking, and AI assistance into one seamless platform—completely free and open-source.
